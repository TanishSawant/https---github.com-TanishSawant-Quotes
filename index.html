En boca cerrada no entran moscas

Meaning: Flies don't enter a closed mouth. (You won't make a mistake if you don't talk.)

La cabra siempre tira al monte.

Meaning: The goat always heads toward the mountain. (The leopard doesn't change its spots. You can't teach an old dog new tricks.)

Dame pan y dime tonto.

Meaning: Give me bread and call me a fool. (Think of me what you will. As long as I get what I want, it doesn't matter what you think.)

Borra con el codo lo que escribe con la mano

He/she erases with the elbow what his/her hand is writing. (Whatever good actions or decisions he makes, he invalidates by other actions)

A los tontos no les dura el dinero.

Money does not last for fools. (A fool and his money are soon parted.)

El amor todo lo puede.

Love can do it all. (Love will find a way.)
